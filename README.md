## About Us

Oggel Codelabs is a specialized Laravel development consultancy focused on helping scale-ups (2-12 developers) in the Netherlands and beyond professionalize their Laravel applications. We combine senior-level technical expertise with strategic thinking to deliver measurable improvements in deployment frequency, lead time, and code quality.

**Our Mission**: Transform technical debt into scalable architecture, slow deploys into rapid iterations, and team chaos into structured workflows.

## What We Do

### Core Services

-   **Laravel Professionalization**: Strategic refactoring and architecture improvements
-   **DORA Metrics Implementation**: Measurable DevOps improvements (Deployment Frequency, Lead Time, MTTR, Change Failure Rate)
-   **Technical Debt Reduction**: Tackle legacy code without breaking everything
-   **Team Coaching**: Best practices implementation and workflow optimization
-   **CI/CD Pipeline Setup**: From manual deploys to automated, zero-downtime deployments

### Specializations

-   **Scale-up Focus**: Teams experiencing growth pain (technical debt, slow deploys, team chaos)
-   **DORA Metrics**: Help teams move from DORA laggards to high performers
-   **Strategic Refactoring**: Incremental improvements that don't slow down development
-   **Test Coverage**: Building confidence through comprehensive test suites
